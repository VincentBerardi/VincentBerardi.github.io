City Defense

Safeguard the Realm!
(Concordia Team Project)

Safeguard The Realm! is a 2.5D tower defense game made in Unity submitted for Concordia's COMP 376 class. You defend the castle against waves of enemies, by placing various types of defence towers on the grid map after a market phase.
